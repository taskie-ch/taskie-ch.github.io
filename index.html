Task sharing with ease

Uniquely task manager which simplifies your tasks.

Instead creating notes on the fridge or excel sheets who has what to do, you can use taskie. The application will ask you the name of the task and how frequent it has to be done if it is recurrent. You can assign, skip and reschedule tasks. Taskie is the solution you searched for to never miss a assignment again.

Scheduling when you want

Let taskie know about your absences.

taskie has an intelligent scheduling algorithm, which reassigns or reschedules tasks which are assigned to you during your absences. It distributes the workload automatically, so no roommate is threat unfairly.

Notifications

Never miss a due date again.

taskie will notify you on due dates of your tasks. Also if you are skipping your tasks multiple times, taskie will send a notification to your roommates. Who wants to have lazy roommates? If you are upset because a roommate forgot about a task, you an notify him by yourself over taskie.

Hall Of Fame

Let's play a game.

Whenever you finish a task, you got the score of the particular task. This sums up to your monthly score, which in the end will be represented in the HallOfFame page of your flat. Try to engage all your roomies that they contribute as much as possible. Maybe you can agree on a price for the top player by end of the month?
